Matthew Xavier Corrigan

making songs happen

Matthew Xavier Corrigan is a musician, artist, art enthusiast et cetera from Cork City, Ireland. Matthew’s practice is primarily concerned with the performance, staging and production of music. There is other practice also.

Note on original music

Matthew’s history is primarily with original music.

  • performs live with alex gough
  • regular performances under other names/bands
  • involved in foundation and maintenance of hausu / important! audio arts collectives
  • guitarist / bassist / producer / mix engineer / live electronics / vocalist / other things

Note on producing and curating

Matthew aims also to facilitate the work of others as often as possible, through the production of live music events, exhibitions and art installations, such as COLLECTIVE COLLABORATION at the 2022 Rebound Arts Festival alongside various other outlets.
